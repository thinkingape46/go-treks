Treks We Offer

Our treks takes you to the places you will remember in the years to come. We provide the services that make the whole time of yours trip smooth and enjoyable.

Kumaraparvatha

Kumaraparvatha / Pushpagiri is the highest peak in Pushpagiri Wildlife Sanctuary in the Western Ghats.It is located in the northern part of Kodagu district. It is known for one of the hardest in Karnataka.

Trek Distance: 18km (ascent and descent)

Climb: 800m

Trip Duration: 1 day and 2 nights

Kudremukha

Kudremukh is one of the famous peak for trekking located in Chikkamagaluru district, in Karnataka, India. Located around 10 kms from the town of Kalasa is a peak you should not miss in Karnataka.

Trek Distance: 22km (ascent and descent)

Climb: 1600m

Trip Duration: 2 days and 2 nights

Frequently Asked Questions

Our answers to the frequently answered questions.

What is your base city?

We are based in Bangalore.

How do I know whether my trip is confirmed?

You will find the details under the trip tab in your account section, we also send you a confirmation mail. If any problems, please reach us through phone.

Can I cancel the order and get a refund?

Yes, it is possible. Please do cancel atleast three days before your the trip date. e.g. If the trip starts on 10th, cancel it by 7th.

What is covered under each order?

We cover transport, food, accomodation and any ticket charges.

Are children allowed?

As all our trips involve some kind of physical activity like walking, our trips are suitable for children over 13 years and they should be under the guidance of an adult.
